Resistors and Springs

Question

What is the effective resistance of two resistors in series?
What is the effective spring constant two springs in series?
Why are these different?

Hints

Think about what quantities are conserved in series.

Answer

For resistors in series, the current through the circuit is conserved, and voltage is added. $V=IR$ For springs in series, the force is conserved, and extension is added. $F=kx$
